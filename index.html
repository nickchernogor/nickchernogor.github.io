Hello, my name is Nick Chernogor!

Based in New York City, I'm a recent graduate of Cornell University's College of Arts and Sciences, having earned a Bachelor of Arts with a double major in Computer Science and Linguistics.

My interests lie in the intersection of these two fields, exploring computational representations of human language. I am also passionate about the use of technology for social impact. I hope to apply these interests to a career in software engineering.

Work Experience

In this role, I utilized Java with Spring Boot and Maven and contributed features to a digital asset management service.

In the spring of 2024, I served as a Teaching Assistant for CS/INFO 4300: Language and Information, individually helping students with Python programming and course concepts including information retrieval and machine learning, as well as serving as TA advisor for a group of students developing a final project incorporating these concepts.

Research Experience

Check out our paper or play a web game I created to gather data!
