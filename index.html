Hello, my name is Nick Chernogor!

Originally from Queens, NY, I'm a junior at Cornell University's College of Arts and Sciences pursuing a Bachelor of Arts with a double major in Computer Science and Linguistics. My interests lie in the intersection of these two fields, exploring computational representations of human language. I hope to apply these interests to a career in software engineering, natural language processing, or data science.

I do research under Professor Cristian Danescu-Niculescu-Mizil on conversation analysis and forecasting, working on identifying tension in online discussions and predicting antisocial outcomes, as well as communicating such predictions to discussion participants in helpful ways.

I spend my free time maintaining my Duolingo streak (in an effort to learn Ukrainian and Hungarian), reading novels (especially magical realism and Russian classics), painting with watercolors, and going on walks, hikes, or bike rides around gorgeous Ithaca, NY.
